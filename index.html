PEMDAS Calculator

See a need, fill a need! ‐ BigWeld Industries.

That is from the 2005 film called Robots. If you ever need a simple place to compute arithmetic without using parentheses, this is the place for you!

I, initally, thought the Odin Project requires us to make a PEMDAS calculator, but I didn't read that thoroughly and ended up making this one. I remember telling myself, "Why are we being instructed to calculate incorrectly?" Only to realize too late that it was deliberately designed that way. But hey, making a PEMDAS calculator was challenging and fun!
